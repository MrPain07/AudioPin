AudioPin — Privacy Policy

AudioPin runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. It makes no network connections of any kind.

Your rules, settings, and activity log are saved locally on your computer (in %APPDATA%\AudioPin) and never leave it. There are no accounts, no telemetry, no analytics, and no third-party services.

Purchases and trials are handled entirely by the Microsoft Store; AudioPin itself receives no personal information from them.
