Windows forgets where your audio belongs. AudioPin doesn't.

Pin any app to any output device.

One rule: this app, this device. Enforced silently from the system tray — through app restarts, default changes, and device chaos.

Self-healing.

Unplug a headset or an HDMI display and plug it back in — AudioPin moves your audio home within seconds, live, without restarting the app.

Strict pins.

Flag a rule as strict and the app goes silent when its device is missing — instead of blasting through your speakers at 2 AM.

Guard device.

Protect one output. If it disconnects, every other output is muted until it returns. Nothing leaks, anywhere.

Nothing in your audio path.

No virtual devices, no drivers, no mixer, zero added latency. Native Windows routing — AudioPin just makes it keep its promises.
